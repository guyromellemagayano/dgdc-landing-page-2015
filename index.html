Jojie Alcantara

Photography

She is a TV host, a Davao-based journalist, multi-awarded photographer and artist. Her works have been featured in tourism campaigns, covers and spreads of books and international magazines. Her personal advocacy is to promote her country positively to the world, showcasing Philippines' natural attractions.


Harvey Tolibao

Comic Illustration

Harvey Montecillo Tolibao hails from Malaybalay, Bukidnon. A furniture designer, cartoonist, graphic artist, illustrator, web designer and tattoo artist. He was determined to pursue his dream of becoming a comic book artist, and he became a freelance artist for Marvel comics. He eventually paved his way to get a chance to work with Green Arrow series with writer Ann Nocenti.

About Graphicon


  • 2011
  • 2012
  • 2013

Graphic Design Conference, or simply Graphicon, is the hottest and the most anticipated premiere graphic design event in Davao City. Now on its fourth iteration, Graphicon attracts professionals in the worlds of information technology, graphic design and art as well as students and enthusiasts from various fields. Graphicon aims to provide further awareness of graphic design and multimedia while improving the state of the field here in Davao City and the surrounding regions by providing a venue for individuals to learn from the best in the industry, meet like-minded individuals who can help them in their journey to being better artists and interact with companies and organizations who can support them.


At the heart of Graphicon are the series of lectures delivered by our invited speakers, made up of leaders in their respective creative fields. Our speakers who came from all over the Philippines are invited to talk about their beginnings and industry experience, as well as to impart tips on how to succeed in the creative arena. This year, we are proud to have with us the following giants in the contemporary Philippine design scene:


  • Harvey Tolibao - Comic Illustration
  • Lester Cruz & Carlo Castillo - Branding and Design
  • Jojie Alcantara - Photography
  • Patrick Cabral - Typography
  • Marvin Te - Animation
  • April San Pedro - Paper Crafting
